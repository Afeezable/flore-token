## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can more tokens be created after deployment?
No. The smart contract does not include minting functionality. Total supply is permanently fixed.

### Are there any token burns?
There are no automatic burn mechanisms. Any burns would be manual and publicly announced.

### How can I verify vesting schedules?
All vesting contracts are publicly viewable on Etherscan. Check the token's top holders page and contract interactions.

### Is FLORE an investment?
No. FLORE is a governance utility token for a 501(c)(3) nonprofit. It does not represent an investment or promise of returns.

### How do I participate in governance?
Token holders can vote on proposals through the Foundation's governance portal. Details available in the [Governance Guide](./docs/governance.md).
